Welcome

Hello and welcome! I am Arka Prava Basu , undergraduate Computer Science student from NIT Durgapur. I have always been enthusiastic about coding and after joining college I have been attracted to software development. I use my leisure time in developing Android applications. While developing I try to think out of the box, trying to solve the problems which I face handling any computerised device. I also dive into competitive coding from time to time.

I am also an avid gamer and in a way it has attracted me in Game Development. While developing games I generally work in a team of three. As a game developer I mainly work on level designing and game AI. I am currently working in a FPS shooter game which , hopefully, will release mid next year.
